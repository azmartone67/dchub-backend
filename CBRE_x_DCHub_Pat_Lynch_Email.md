## DESIGN RATIONALE

- **Subject line "Picking your brain on H2 2026"** — friend-language, not sales-language. Higher open rate than any pitch-y subject.
- **Opens with proof of work** — naming the two CBRE reports + four specific 2026 Outlook trends signals respect for his work.
- **Phase 1 = zero-friction** — citation only, no money. Pat-discretion only.
- **Phases 2 + 3 framed as "if that lands"** — no presumption.
- **Slide 4 callout** — directs him to a specific 60-second-read page rather than "review the whole deck."
- **"I'll come to you"** — removes scheduling friction.
- **No corporate sign-off** — friends don't write business signatures.
