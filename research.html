Methodology

Data Sources & Enrichment Pipeline

Four distinct acquisition pipelines, each attacking permit data from a different angle with confidence scoring to prevent lower-quality data from overwriting higher-quality records.

📄

SEC / EDGAR Filings

10-K annual reports from public REITs (Equinix, Digital Realty, Iron Mountain, CoreSite). Item 2 "Properties" sections are parsed across 8 years of filings to find the first year a market appeared — establishing an "operational by" date.

Confidence 0.75 – 0.92
● Live
🏛️

County Permit Portals

BuildingPermit.io API (~75% US county coverage) plus direct county portal integrations for Virginia, Texas, and Georgia — the three highest-density DC markets. Address-matched to facilities with fuzzy scoring.

Confidence 0.65 – 0.85
◐ Expanding
🔍

FOIA Requests

Freedom of Information Act requests to municipal building departments for non-public operators. High-fidelity full-date records including certificate of occupancy dates. Slower cadence, gold-standard accuracy.

Confidence 0.90 – 1.00
◐ In development
⚡

AI-Assisted Extraction

Press releases, permitting announcements, and news articles processed by DC Hub's AI pipeline. Extracts facility names, addresses, and commissioning dates. Cross-referenced against existing records before writing.

Confidence 0.55 – 0.75
