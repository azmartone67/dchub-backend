## Why this works

- **Specific numbers first** (96 platforms, 12,877 facilities, 20
  partners) — earns the scroll-stop
- **Concrete value props** (live profile, attribution, AI-platform
  discovery, persistent inbound) — bullet list, not vague
- **No "delve" / "moreover" / "unleash"** — DC Hub voice rules
- **One clear CTA** — submit at /partners
- **Three hashtags max in the short variant** — algorithm preference
