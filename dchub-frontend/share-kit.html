How to share — the structure that works

  • Lead with the third-party validation (the Gemini quote, the registries listing us). Self-praise has a ceiling; "AI engines cite us" doesn't.
  • Always include a live link — /cited-by, /reports/monthly, /brain. Real-time data beats screenshots.
  • Pair the number with the proof: not "21K calls" but "21K calls (verifiable at /cited-by)".
  • For partnerships, give a low-commitment ask: 30- or 90-day pilots beat "let's talk strategy". The one-pagers already do this.
  • For HN/Reddit, lead with what's open: open DCPI methodology + open citation telemetry. The Show-HN crowd respects transparency.
