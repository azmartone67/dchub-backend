Timeline

Batch Zero Critical Path

ERCOT's first-ever batch allocation of transmission capacity to large loads. Every date a developer needs to know.

Mar 4, 2026
PGRR145 Published
Batch Zero Process for Large Load Interconnections formally proposed. Replaces sequential LLIS with coordinated, system-wide study.
May 1, 2026
Stability Assessment Deadline (Path 1)
Projects must be included in a Quarterly Stability Assessment or interim ride-through assessment to qualify.
May 2026
PRS / TAC / Board Votes
Critical governance votes. Approval authorizes Batch Zero implementation with target effective date of August 1.
Jul 15, 2026
Developer Information Deadline
Technical specs, site preparation attestations, and equipment procurement evidence due. Projects targeting Dec 2027 energization must demonstrate progress.
Aug 1, 2026
Batch Zero Effective Date
Full Batch Study rules and Batch Zero process take effect.
Early 2027
Preliminary MW Allocations
Year-by-year reliably served MW issued (2028–2032). Capacity based on transmission security analysis at each POI.
Mar 2027
Commitment Deadline
30 days after results to commit or withdraw. Financial responsibility locked. Withdrawn capacity not reallocated within Batch Zero.
2028–2032
Phased Capacity Delivery
Years 1–5 limited by existing transmission. Year 6+ assumes upgrades from RTP/RPG. Partial allocations common in constrained corridors.
