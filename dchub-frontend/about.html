Our Mission
Democratizing Data Center Intelligence
Enterprise-grade data center intelligence has historically been locked behind $11,000-$100,000/year consulting contracts and fragmented across dozens of sources. DC Hub changes that.

We aggregate, enrich, and deliver real-time intelligence on every data center facility, M&A transaction, capacity expansion, and market trend — accessible via an interactive platform, REST API, and AI agent integrations. Our autonomous intelligence systems scan 60+ news sources every minute, extracting structured data from unstructured announcements using deep learning.

Whether you're a site selection consultant evaluating power availability, an investor tracking M&A deal flow, or a hyperscaler planning your next campus — DC Hub gives you the data to make better decisions, faster.
