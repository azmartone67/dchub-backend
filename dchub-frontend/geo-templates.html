Major Providers

Power & Infrastructure

Silicon Valley faces power constraints due to high demand. PG&E requires extensive planning for large deployments (50MW+). California's aggressive renewable mandates drive sustainability focus with 50%+ renewable energy mix.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many data centers are in Silicon Valley?

DC Hub tracks 150+ data center facilities in the Silicon Valley/Bay Area market, with a combined capacity exceeding 800 MW. Major clusters include Santa Clara, San Jose, and Fremont/Milpitas.

What is the average power rate in Silicon Valley?

Commercial power rates average $0.12-$0.18/kWh through PG&E, making it one of the higher-cost markets in the US. Many operators secure power through long lead-time utility negotiations or on-site generation.
